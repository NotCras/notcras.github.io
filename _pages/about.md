Currently a Doctoral Candidate at Oregon State University under the advisory directions of Cindy Grimm and Ravi Balasubramanian. I work on evaluating physical robot hand designs with human studies. 

My past work has been rather interdisciplinary. I currently hold a Masters from OSU under the advisory direction of Yigit Menguc. In my thesis, I demonstrate successful 3D printing of silicone elastomer soft robots. My undergraduate is from Worcester Polytechnic Institute for Robotics Engineering. I participated in research there in developing a robot hand with soft robot fingers with integrated sensing and embedded hard components for grasping.

Outside of my research, I am a husband to a wonderful wife and father of a three month son. I enjoy playing table tennis (although my skills are quite lacking) as well as reading books and watching film (both animated and live-action).
